# Assumptions

## Modeling Assumptions

- returns before retirement are fixed at one annual rate
- returns during retirement are fixed at one annual rate
- contributions are monthly and can grow annually
- withdrawals can be monthly, quarterly, or annual
- retirement spending increases by inflation plus any extra annual withdrawal increase

## Product Assumptions

- the MVP is anonymous and local-first
- scenario persistence is browser-only
- the app is US-dollar oriented
- the app favors understandable deterministic outputs before advanced simulation

## Known Omissions

- taxes
- investment fees
- Social Security
- pensions
- one-time deposits or expenses
- spouse or joint planning
- probability-based simulation

## Communication Rules

- results must be presented as estimates
- the UI must state that this is not financial advice
- assumptions should be visible near the results, not hidden behind a separate flow
